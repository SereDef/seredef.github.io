  • Common misconceptions about multiple imputation | Slides
    Tags: Selection bias Predictive mean matching Imputation quality control Data simulation

    Multiple imputation is a very useful approach to selection bias, but it is rarely used in epidemiological research due to some common misconceptions. In this talk we go over these ideas and disprove them. We also introduce multiple imputation and provide practical tutorial on how to implement this in R.
